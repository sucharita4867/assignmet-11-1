Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new and impactful solutions that improve lives and drive progress.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to thrive.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the essential systems and structures, like roads, power, and communication networks, that support society and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving people the confidence, skills, and resources to take control of their lives and achieve their goals.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for
all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"The vision of the future is a world shaped by technology, innovation, and sustainability. Smart cities will enhance daily life through automation, AI, and clean energy solutions. Education and digital transformation will empower individuals, creating equal opportunities for all. Businesses will embrace eco-friendly practices, ensuring a balance between progress and environmental care. Healthcare and communication will advance, making services more accessible and efficient. A future built on collaboration, creativity, and inclusivity will drive humanity toward a better, connected world."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
